What is Cost Plus Pricing?

Cost Plus is a transparent pricing model where you pay the actual cost of services (tools, content creation, link building, freelancers, etc.) plus a fixed percentage markup for our management, expertise, and overhead.

  • Full Transparency: We share all invoices, receipts, and expenses with you monthly
  • Better Value: No inflated pricing - you see exactly what services cost
  • Trust-Based: Perfect for long-term partnerships built on transparency
  • Flexible Budget: Scale services up or down based on actual needs

Important: The monthly service fee covers our time for management, reporting, strategy, and client communication. All direct costs (tools, vendors, freelancers) are billed at actual cost + markup percentage.
